Blog to Book

Transform your online writings into a timeless book

How does it work?

  • You have a website with posts, articles, etc.
  • You give us some info
  • We take your writings and format it into a book
  • We handle all the editing and formatting, so you don't have to. If we have any questions, we will email you
  • It is then sent to blurb.com to be printed
  • Your finished book is shipped to you and will be in your hands within a couple weeks!
  • More questions? feel free to contact us!

Pricing

  • 30-50 pages for $30 (must have at least 30 pages)
  • All options come in books sized 5x7 with colored pages and a white cover (We will have more options in the future).

Get your book started now!

First, pick a starting place for your book. Then choose the number of pages you want to fill up. We will take your writings from that date and go post to post until the pages are all filled up. If you want 30 pages, and your post needs to extend to 31 pages, you will get that page for free.

For example, say you want to get posts starting from March 2016 and only want to spend $50. Enter March 2016, then put 50 as the page count.
